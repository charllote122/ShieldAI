About ShieldAI

Building safer digital spaces for women and girls across Africa through AI and compassion

Our Mission

ShieldAI was born from a simple but powerful vision: every woman and girl deserves to participate in digital spaces without fear of violence, harassment, or abuse.

In Africa, where digital adoption is growing rapidly, women face unique challenges online. From gender-based harassment to culturally-specific forms of digital violence, the need for protection is urgent and real.

We combine cutting-edge artificial intelligence with deep cultural understanding to create protection that actually works for African women.
