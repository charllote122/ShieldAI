Your Rights Under Kenyan Law:

  • Computer Misuse and Cybercrimes Act: Protects against online harassment
  • Data Protection Act: Safeguards your personal information
  • Sexual Offences Act: Covers online sexual harassment
  • Protection Against Domestic Violence Act: Includes digital abuse

How to Take Legal Action:

  1. Document all evidence with timestamps
  2. Report to nearest police station
  3. Contact Kenya Cybercrime Unit
  4. Seek legal aid from FIDA Kenya or Kituo Cha Sheria
